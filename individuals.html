Our Purpose

60 Point Capital partners with individuals and families navigating significant, multigenerational wealth. We serve as trusted stewards of your legacy, providing the strategic guidance and specialized expertise needed to preserve and grow wealth across generations. Our commitment extends beyond investment returns—we help you align your financial strategy with your values, navigate complex decisions, and build a lasting legacy that reflects your family's priorities and aspirations.

Why Work With Us

We understand that significant wealth requires more than traditional portfolio management. As individuals and families, you need a partner who combines deep investment expertise with a nuanced understanding of wealth stewardship, generational transitions, and legacy planning.

Our tailored approach provides access to sophisticated investment opportunities—private credit, secondaries, and co-investments—typically reserved for institutional investors. We deliver these opportunities with the personalized service, discretion, and strategic guidance that families require. Whether you're exploring alternative investments, navigating generational transitions, or seeking to align your portfolio with your values, we provide the expertise and partnership you need to achieve your objectives.

What We Do

Our services are designed to meet the complex needs of individuals and families with significant wealth. We combine investment expertise with strategic planning and personalized service to deliver comprehensive solutions.

Custom Portfolios

We design and manage custom investment portfolios tailored to your unique circumstances, goals, and risk tolerance. Our approach integrates traditional and alternative investments, including private credit, secondaries, and co-investments, to build diversified portfolios that align with your objectives.

Investment Strategy & Management

Our team develops and executes investment strategies that reflect your values and objectives. We provide ongoing oversight, strategic guidance, and proactive management to help you navigate market opportunities and challenges while maintaining a focus on long-term wealth preservation and growth.

Capital Solutions

We help you access sophisticated capital solutions, including structured credit, private debt, and alternative financing options. Whether you're seeking income generation, portfolio diversification, or strategic capital deployment, we identify and structure opportunities that meet your needs.

Risk Management

We implement comprehensive risk management frameworks designed to protect your wealth while enabling growth. Our approach includes portfolio diversification, liquidity planning, insurance strategies, and cybersecurity measures to safeguard your assets and information.

OCIO and Consulting

For families seeking comprehensive investment oversight, we provide Outsourced Chief Investment Officer (OCIO) services. Our consulting approach helps you evaluate opportunities, structure transactions, and build investment programs that align with your family's values and objectives.
