How We Work

IPS

Define objectives, risk limits, liquidity buckets, ranges, and rebalance rules. Agree escalation thresholds.

Pipeline

Source and score opportunities. Stage-gate with screening notes, DD checklists, and pricing memos.

Execution

Negotiate terms, allocate within limits, monitor exposures, and report with exception flags.
