Our Purpose

60 Point Capital partners with family offices managing the financial, legal and personal affairs of wealthy families. We serve as strategic partners, providing the scale, technology, and operational infrastructure needed to support and evolve family enterprises. Our commitment extends beyond investment management—we help you build robust systems, navigate complex governance structures, and create frameworks that enable your family office to operate with efficiency, discretion, and long-term sustainability.

Why Work With Us

Family offices face unique challenges balancing the needs of multiple stakeholders, generational transitions, and complex governance structures. As a family office, you need a partner who combines deep investment expertise with operational sophistication and institutional-grade infrastructure.

We bring scale, technology, and operational capabilities that enable family offices to operate more effectively. Our team works as an extension of your family office, providing the investment expertise, strategic guidance, and operational support needed to navigate complex decisions, structure transactions, and build investment programs that align with your family's values, objectives, and risk profile. Whether you're building new capabilities, scaling existing operations, or navigating generational transitions, we provide the infrastructure and expertise needed to support and evolve your family enterprise.

What We Do

Our services are designed to meet the complex needs of family offices managing significant wealth across multiple generations. We combine investment expertise with operational sophistication to deliver comprehensive solutions.

Custom Portfolios

We design and manage custom investment portfolios tailored to your family's unique circumstances, objectives, and risk tolerance. Our approach integrates traditional and alternative investments, including private credit, secondaries, and co-investments, to build diversified portfolios that align with your family's values and long-term objectives.

Investment Strategy & Management

Our team develops and executes investment strategies that reflect your family's values and objectives. We provide ongoing oversight, strategic guidance, and proactive management to help you navigate market opportunities and challenges while maintaining a focus on long-term wealth preservation and growth.

Capital Solutions

We help you access sophisticated capital solutions, including structured credit, private debt, and alternative financing options. Whether you're seeking income generation, portfolio diversification, or strategic capital deployment for family-owned businesses, we identify and structure opportunities that meet your needs.

Risk Management

We implement comprehensive risk management frameworks designed to protect your family's wealth while enabling growth. Our approach includes portfolio diversification, liquidity planning, insurance strategies, and cybersecurity measures to safeguard your assets and information.

OCIO and Consulting

For family offices seeking comprehensive investment oversight, we provide Outsourced Chief Investment Officer (OCIO) services. Our consulting approach helps you evaluate opportunities, structure transactions, and build investment programs that align with your family's values and objectives while providing the operational support needed to execute effectively.
