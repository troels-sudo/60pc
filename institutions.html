Long-term partners to mission-driven institutions.

60 Point Capital works with foundations, endowments, pensions, corporates, and sovereign investment arms that require transparent governance and measurable impact. We embed clarity, cadence, and accountability inside institutional portfolios. Our team becomes an extension of your staff—crafting investment policies, preparing board materials, and running disciplined manager selection and co-investment processes. The outcome is a program that withstands leadership transitions, regulatory reviews, and market cycles while staying anchored to mission.
