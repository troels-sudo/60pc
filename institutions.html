Our Purpose

60 Point Capital partners with mission-driven foundations, endowments, pensions and corporates seeking strategic financial oversight and governance support. We serve as strategic partners, helping institutional investors build resilient portfolios and achieve long-term impact while maintaining rigorous governance and risk management standards. Our commitment extends beyond investment returns—we help you align your investment strategy with your mission, navigate complex fiduciary responsibilities, and build programs that deliver sustainable, long-term value for your stakeholders and beneficiaries.

Why Work With Us

Institutional investors operate in a complex environment of fiduciary responsibilities, regulatory requirements, and stakeholder expectations. As a foundation, endowment, pension, or corporate investment program, you need a partner who combines deep investment expertise with institutional-grade governance, strategic oversight, and long-term perspective.

We bring the strategic oversight and governance support needed to navigate these complexities while achieving long-term impact. Our team works with institutional investment committees and boards to develop investment strategies, evaluate opportunities, and implement programs that align with your mission, risk tolerance, and return objectives. Whether you're building new investment programs, scaling existing operations, or navigating regulatory changes, we provide the expertise, governance frameworks, and operational support needed to build and maintain institutional-quality investment programs that deliver sustainable, long-term value.

What We Do

Our services are designed to meet the complex needs of institutional investors operating in environments with significant fiduciary responsibilities and regulatory requirements. We combine investment expertise with governance sophistication to deliver comprehensive solutions.

Custom Portfolios

We design and manage custom investment portfolios tailored to your institutional objectives, constraints, and risk tolerance. Our approach integrates traditional and alternative investments, including private credit, secondaries, and co-investments, to build diversified portfolios that align with your mission and long-term objectives while meeting fiduciary standards.

Investment Strategy & Management

Our team develops and executes investment strategies that reflect your institutional mission, objectives, and constraints. We provide ongoing oversight, strategic guidance, and proactive management to help you navigate market opportunities and challenges while maintaining a focus on long-term value creation and alignment with your fiduciary responsibilities.

Capital Solutions

We help you access sophisticated capital solutions, including structured credit, private debt, and alternative financing options. Whether you're seeking income generation, portfolio diversification, or strategic capital deployment, we identify and structure opportunities that meet your institutional needs while maintaining appropriate governance and risk management standards.

Risk Management

We implement comprehensive risk management frameworks designed to protect your institutional assets while enabling growth. Our approach includes portfolio diversification, liquidity planning, regulatory compliance, and governance frameworks to safeguard your investments and ensure alignment with fiduciary responsibilities.

OCIO and Consulting

For institutions seeking comprehensive investment oversight, we provide Outsourced Chief Investment Officer (OCIO) services. Our consulting approach helps you evaluate opportunities, structure transactions, and build investment programs that align with your institutional mission and objectives while providing the governance support and operational infrastructure needed to execute effectively.
